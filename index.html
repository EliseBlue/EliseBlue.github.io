The Architect Within

From insight to mind

Mind to hand

Hand to compass

Compass to carbon

And carbon to vellum

The passion,

The dove,

Soon rises above

From quarry to stone

Brick to mason

Mason to mortar

And mortar to pillar

The labor

The love

Soon rises above

Tis the mind which the architect relies

Only for work he gives

His love he denies

Wanting out

Desiring

And demanding

Demanding, Demanding, Demanding

Through disaster

Weather

War

And time

By Steven Douglas Hail
